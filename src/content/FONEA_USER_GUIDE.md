## ğŸ†˜ Troubleshooting

### Songs Won't Import?
- Make sure you're copying the full JSON from Fonea Companion GPT
- Check that the JSON is valid (no cut-off text)
- Try importing fewer songs at once (max 25 recommended)

### Song Not Verified?
- Some very new or obscure songs may not be in MusicBrainz yet
- You can still manually search for them on streaming platforms
- Click the platform search buttons to find them

### Can't Export to Spotify?
- Make sure you're logged into Spotify in Fonea (Settings â†’ Connect Spotify)
- Check that your playlist has at least one song
- Try refreshing your Spotify connection

### Preview Won't Play?
- Check your browser allows audio playback
- Some songs may not have preview URLs available
- Try a different browser if issues persist
